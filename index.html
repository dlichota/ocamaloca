Oca Maloca vacation rental apartment on Tortola, British Virgin Islands is 5 minutes from Nanny Cay Marina and overlooks Sir Francis Drake Channel and the Caribbean Sea, with a view of Norman Island and Peter Island and other islands of the BVI and USVI. Oca Maloca is an airconditioned, one bedroom, one bathroom apartment and is well appointed with a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, free wifi and parking. There is a sofa bed for a third person to sleep and a large wrap-around deck from which to enjoy meals, sip cocktails and relish the view.


What does Oca Maloca mean?
An Oca Maloca is an ancestral long house used by indigenous people of the Amazon.
