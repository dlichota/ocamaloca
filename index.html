Villa Oca Maloca
Vacation Apartment
Near Nanny Cay Marina
Tortola, British Virgin Islands

Oca Maloca vacation rental apartment on Tortola, British Virgin Islands is 5 minutes from Nanny Cay Marina and overlooks Sir Francis Drake Channel and the Caribbean Sea, with a view of Norman Island and Peter Island and other islands of the BVI and USVI. Oca Maloca is an airconditioned, one bedroom, one bathroom apartment and is well appointed with a fully equipped gourmet kitchen. There is a sofa bed for a third person to sleep and a large wrap-around deck from which to sip cocktails and enjoy the view.

The owners have lived and worked on Tortola for many years and are very familar with the best beaches, bars, restaurants and shopping. Contact us for any questions you may have about your trip - we are sure to be able to help.
