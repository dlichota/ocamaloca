Experience the Caribbean in ultimate luxury with Oca Maloca, a one-bedroom apartment in Tortola, British Virgin Islands. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea and Sir Francis Drake Channel, just 5 minutes away from the Nanny Cay Marina. This air-conditioned apartment features a fully equipped gourmet kitchen, free WiFi connection, and a large wrap-around deck with mesmerizing views of Norman Island, Peter Island, and other islands of the BVI and USVI. Perfect for a couple or a small group of friends, the sofa bed provides extra sleeping space. Don't miss out on this highly sought-after Caribbean rental apartment and book your stay now!
